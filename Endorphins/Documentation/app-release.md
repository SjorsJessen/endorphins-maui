# Release app on MacOS/iOS

To run a personal, unreleased Apple app on your iPad, sideload the .ipa file using a computer or a direct-install
service. Sideloading means installing an app outside the official App Store. This process requires an installation tool,
a signing certificate, and permission from your iPad.

## Method 1: Using a ComputerThis method is free and uses your

This method is free and uses your personal Apple ID. 
You must connect your iPad to a computer to refresh the app every 7 days.

1. Download the app’s .ipa file to your computer;
2. Download and open [Sideloadly](https://sideloadly.io/);
3. Connect your iPad to your computer with a USB cable;
4. Enter your Apple ID and drag the .ipa file into Sideloadly;
5. Click Start to install the app

## Method 2: Without a ComputerThis method requires

This method requires third-party tools to sign and install apps directly from your Safari browser without connecting to a computer.

1. Download an on-device signing tool like [SideStore](https://sidestore.io/) or follow community guides on [GitHub](https://github.com/opsec-bot/IOS-Sideloading-No-Jailbreak);
2. Import your Apple ID or a valid signing certificate into the tool;
3. Download the .ipa file to your iPad and open it inside your signing tool;
4. Tap Install

## Trusting

When you open a sideloaded app for the first time, your iPad will block it for security reasons.

1. Go to *Settings > General > VPN & Device Management*;
2. Tap your Apple ID under the Developer App section;
3. Tap Trust [Your Name]
